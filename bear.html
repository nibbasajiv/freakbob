Meet Bear!

Birthday: July 17, 2013

Hi, I'm Bear — a dog with a big heart and an even bigger appetite!

I live for food. No, really. If you open a bag of chips, I will hear it from a mile away. Pizza crust? Mine. That thing you dropped on the floor 2 days ago? Still mine.

When I'm not busy being a professional snack hunter, I enjoy long walks where I pretend I'm on a secret mission. I sniff everything. It's all very classified.

Sometimes I chase squirrels. They never RSVP to my parties though.

Thanks for visiting my rad site! Don't forget to feed your dog. Or me. Especially me.
